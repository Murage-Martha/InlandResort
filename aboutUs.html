Who we are.

Nice to meet you

Our story begins 15 years ago, with a vision of a refuge in nature. Our founder, while on a camping trip, dreamt of a sanctuary away from the city life. His love for nature brought this dream into fruition, a seed that grew into the now SereneStay resort.

At SereneStay we take pride in our commitment to providing a unique and peaceful experience for all our guests. We believe in conserving the splendor of our surroundings while operating in an efficient way to reduce our environmental impact. Our rooms are curated to blend effortlessly into the surrounding environment, offering breathtaking scenery.

Our guests love the unique blend of comfort and nature in our accommodations. Whether waking up to sunrise yoga on the outdoor deck, exploring nearby hiking trails, or simply listening to the birdsong from their private patio, every moment fosters a sense of connection with the natural world.
